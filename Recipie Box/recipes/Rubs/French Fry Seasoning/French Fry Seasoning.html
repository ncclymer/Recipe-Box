French Fry Seasoning -- Total time: 5 mins Home

 Ingredients:
  • 1/4 tsp ground white pepper
  • 1/4 tsp ground cayenne
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp iodzied table salt
  • 1/4 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground oregano
Method:
  • For the best result put all ingredients into an air tight container and shake until combined. If there is no container available, then simply place into a bowl and stir together until combined. If you happen to empty out a spice jar take a moment to wash it out and use it as a shaker once it's dry.
